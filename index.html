RPS Demo

Score

Controls:
0 - Bet Red
1 - Bet Green
2 - Bet Blue

Instructions:
The colored dots compete to convert eachother.
Red captures green, green captures blue, and blue captures red.

Bet on colors to increase your score. Your current bet is shown by the color of your score.
Betting on a color scales your score with the population of that color.
EX: If you bet on green, and green's population increases by 20%, your score will also increase by 20%.
